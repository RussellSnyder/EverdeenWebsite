Everdeen Bio

EVERDEEN is an indie/folk/rock band from Stuttgart, Germany and San Francisco. Comparable to artists such as Yeah Yeah Yeahs, Coldplay, Angus & Julia Stone, the band has recently released their album, PERFECT LINES, produced by BENNO RICHTER. The ten-song album is underpinned by the ethereal and emotional voice of singer SÜMEYRA, accompanied by the manifold guitar textures of IAN STAHL. Everdeen's sound on Perfect Lines is marked by dynamics, and ranges from delicate, sylvan folk of "Don't Look Behind" to the racous indie-rock dissonance of "In A Whisper."

In 2011 singer/guitarist Sümeyra met guitarist/multi-instrumentalist Ian while studying music in San Francisco. From the beginning it was clear that they had a creative connection and immediately started writing original material together.

Prior to Everdeen, singer Sümeyra had already established a career as a solo artist, which included the release of an album entitled “THOUGHTS” and a tour throughout southern Germany in 2010. The beginning of her collaboration with Ian coincided with her discovery of an array of new influences which would shape Everdeen's nascent sound.

After relocating to Stuttgart, Germany in early 2014 Everdeen began writing and recording what would become their first full-length album "Perfect Lines". Armed with both classic and cutting-edge influences the group is currently performing shows in the Stuttgart area, accompanied on stage by drummer JOHN WILLIAMSON, bassist RUSSELL SNYDER, and cellist HALINA WILLIAMS.
