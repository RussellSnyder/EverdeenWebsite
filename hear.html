We combine a wide range of sounds to bring you our unique style. We are always working on new material so check back often.

Perfect Lines

Debut album from German/American indie rock band EVERDEEN. Female vocals reminiscent of Adele are accompanied by textural electric guitars. The dynamic arrangements of the album's ten tracks range from a folky whisper to a driving rock 'n' roll roar, often within the same song. released 30 December 2014

See our BandCamp site for more details about the album. If you like it, why don't you buy a copy? If you don't...why not buy a copy for that guy you really hate?

Special thanks to BENNO RICHTER at Jam5 Studio who Produced, engineered, mixed, and mastered this album.
